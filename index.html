Cloud9 is a Non-Profit Organization

Our primary goal is to

empower the women in the society.

Our Team invites you to end violence against women and girls of any form.

Equality between women and men and eliminating all forms of discrimination against women are fundamental human rights. They are universal and enshrined in international law. Despite that, women continue being beaten, exploited, raped, mutilated or forced to marry. They are also being killed only because they are women or girls. The main objective of our NGO is Elimination of Violence against Women, one of the most devastating human rights violations against women and girls around the world. Empowerment of women and girls requires vigorous efforts to bring significant changes in their lives.We say once again, violence must stop now. To this end women need to tell other women that it is right to speak up and that abuse is never, never their fault. Men have a big responsibility to carry. This is courage, this is strength. And we, mothers and fathers, need to tell our daughters that they can be anything they want in life, and we have to tell our sons that protecting and enforcing – that very basic truth - is their duty and responsibility.
